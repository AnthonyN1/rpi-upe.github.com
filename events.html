UPE sponsors a number of events on campus that we believe are of interest to Rensselaer students. This list of events will be updated as we adjust our schedule throughout the semester.

Weekly meetings are held on Tuesdays from 5-6pm in Amos Eaton Room 117

October 24, 2012: UPE Application Meet & Greet with current UPE members:

Come meet current UPE members and learn what being a part of UPE is all about. This event is in AE 117 at 5:30pm.

October 24, 2012: Open Source Software Festival:

The goal is to introduce more people to the world of Open Source Software and spread knowledge to everyone about this phenomenon.

Speakers
We plan to have a series of speakers throughout the day who are willing to give a 20-minute presentation on any topic within the scope of Open Source Software. We are currently looking for volunteer presenters.

Poster Session
If you would like to participate in either, please fill out this form. We will get back to you in a few weeks with a specific presentation time and details on the location.

October 27, 2012: Programming Competition:

Test your programming skills agains the best and brightest RPI has to offer in this semester's Programming Competition. Compete to win prizes that include an iPad, a Nexus 7, and a PlayStation 3! Visit the Programming Competition website for more information.

November 5, 2012 - Noon: Membership applications due:

Make sure you submit your application before the deadline!

November 7, 2012: Notification of acceptance:

After applying, you will be notified on your acceptance by THIS DATE!

November 12, 2012 - 7pm: Induction Ceremony for new members:

After you are accepted, you are invited to the Induction Ceremony where you will officially become a member of UPE.

November TBD, 2012: Semiannual Student and Professor dinner:

Enjoy food, drink, and good times with the Professors of RPI durring the semi-anual Student and Professor Dinner. This traditional event allows UPE members and Professors to discuss acadamics and recreation.
