Tutoring

Upsilon Pi Epsilon is committed to helping Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute students and the surrounding community succeed in the computing and information disciplines. To support this initiative, we have a listserv UPE-Tutors-L@lists.rpi.edu that students can use to contact tutors. We are also developing a website for tutoring that students will be able to use to directly schedule appointments with tutors.
